ENTJ - The Commander

ENTJs are known for their strategic vision and ability to mobilize others. They are natural leaders who excel at setting and achieving goals.

Defining Characteristics

  • Clear vision and goal-oriented
  • Strong leadership and mobilization skills

Cognitive Functions

  • Dominant: Extraverted Thinking (Te)
  • Auxiliary: Introverted Intuition (Ni)
  • Tertiary: Extraverted Sensing (Se)
  • Inferior: Introverted Feeling (Fi)

Productivity Tips for ENTJs

  • Set clear, actionable goals and break them down into manageable tasks.
  • Allow time for strategic thinking and planning.
  • Delegate tasks effectively to leverage team strengths.
